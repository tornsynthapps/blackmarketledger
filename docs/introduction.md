# Introduction to Blackmarket Ledger

Welcome to the **Blackmarket Ledger** documentation! 

Blackmarket Ledger is a comprehensive trading logistics and profit-tracking tool designed specifically for players of [Torn](https://www.torn.com/). It allows you to track your item inventory, calculate cost bases, monitor trading profits, and review loss metrics (such as muggings) to help you make informed trading decisions.

## What is Blackmarket Ledger?

At its core, Blackmarket Ledger provides a centralized dashboard to log your Torn transactions. It calculates:
- **Total Realized Profit**: How much you've made from selling items.
- **Current Inventory Value**: The total cost basis of the items currently in your stock.
- **Total Mug Loss**: Money lost to muggers during your trading activities.
- **Net Total Profit**: Your realized profit minus your mug losses.

## The BML Connect Extension

To make trading even more seamless, we offer the **BML Connect** browser extension (available for Chrome and Firefox). It syncs your cost-basis data directly from the Blackmarket Ledger web app and displays it seamlessly on `torn.com`. This means you can see exactly how much you paid for an item while you're browsing the item market or managing your bazaar, without having to switch tabs.
